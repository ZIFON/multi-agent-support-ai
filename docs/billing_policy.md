# Billing Policy

## Refund Policy

### Refund Window
Customers are eligible for refunds within **14 days** of the original purchase date.

### Refund Processing Times

Refund processing times vary based on the payment method used:

- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-10 business days
  - Refunds are processed by the payment processor
  - Funds will appear on your statement within this timeframe

- **PayPal**: 1-3 business days
  - Typically the fastest refund method
  - Funds are returned to your PayPal account

- **Bank Transfer**: Up to 10 business days
  - Requires bank processing time
  - May take longer depending on your bank

## Subscription Plans

### Plan Types
- **Basic Plan**: $9.99/month
- **Premium Plan**: $29.99/month
- **Enterprise Plan**: $99.99/month

### Plan Renewal
Plans automatically renew on the renewal date unless cancelled.

### Plan Cancellation
You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

## Refund Request Process

1. Open a refund case by providing:
   - Email address
   - Order ID
   - Reason for refund

2. Complete the refund form using the provided link

3. Our team will review and process your request within 2-3 business days

4. Refund will be issued according to the timeline above based on your payment method
